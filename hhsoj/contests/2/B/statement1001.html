Statement

Some people just like being others' fathers. And one day A said to B:"I am your father's father's father" So B will say "..." and just smile. But if "A" said "I am your father's father's son's son's son" So B will be happy and say "ei"(It means "YES" in Chinese) But sometimes A will talk about other things like weather in this situation B will say "nth" Write a program to find out what should B say.

Input format

The first line contains an integer n, the words in a sentence. The next line contains n words, each word contains lower case letters only and the length of each word doesn't exist 100. If the sentence contains "father" and "son" only, calc the meaning of it For all tests 1<=n<=1000

Output format

Print "..." "ei" or "nth" according to the problem statement

Example

        [Input 1]
        3
        father father father
        [Output 1]
        ...
        [Input 2]
        3
        son father son
        [Output 2]
        ei
        [Input 3]
        4
        are you my father
        [Output 3]
        nth
